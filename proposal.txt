<h2>Project: </h2>
<p>PathMan</p>

<h2>Brief Description</h2>
<p>The team will create a game which is designed to help fourthgraders recognize patterns more quickly and easily. </p>

<h2>Team Roles: </h2>
<p>(Tentative) Sarah will design and develop the front end of the program. Ryan will program the engine/back end. Josh will help with front end design, some back end programming, do user testing, and handle documentation. </p>
